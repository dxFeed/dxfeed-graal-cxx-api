\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
Base class for common fields of \mbox{\hyperlink{classTrade}{Trade}}\} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classTradeETH}{Trade\+ETH}} events. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classTrade}{Trade}} events represent the most recent information that is available about the last trade on the market at any given moment of time.

\mbox{\hyperlink{classTrade}{Trade}} event represents last trade information for {\bfseries{regular trading hours}} (RTH) with an official volume and turnover {\bfseries{for the whole trading day}} identified by \mbox{\hyperlink{classTradeBase_a49359e7e646d6916337477a298bef4ea}{day\+Id}} field.

\mbox{\hyperlink{classTradeETH}{Trade\+ETH}} event is defined only for symbols (typically stocks and ETFs) with a designated {\bfseries{extended trading hours}} (ETH, pre market and post market trading sessions). It represents last trade price during ETH and also accumulated volume and turnover during ETH for current trading day.
