\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event is a snapshot of the price and size of the last trade during regular trading hours and an overall day volume and day turnover. 

It represents the most recent information that is available about the regular last trade on the market at any given moment of time.

\doxysubsubsubsubsection*{Trading sessions}

The \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event defines last trade \doxylink{class_trade_base_ac49916e7a2b3d5bfcbbf36d604b7d9f6}{price} as officially defined by the corresponding exchange for its {\bfseries{regular trading hours}} (RTH). It also include an official exchange \doxylink{class_trade_base_a9a956f99368a4bdd97047d3ce8e80790}{day\+Volume} and \doxylink{class_trade_base_a6f9aff4fe560ba262c8bf524274a2408}{day\+Turnover} {\bfseries{for the whole trading day}} identified by \doxylink{class_trade_base_a49359e7e646d6916337477a298bef4ea}{day\+Id}. So, \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event captures all the official numbers that are typically reported by exchange.

Trades that happen in {\bfseries{extended trading hours}} (ETH, pre-\/market and post-\/market trading sessions), which are typically defined for stocks and ETFs, do not update last trade \doxylink{class_trade_base_a42691e5d2128fa83fbe6361975426953}{time}, \doxylink{class_trade_base_a5044e7359f50ff34f59c7ab201b1847b}{exchange\+Code}, \doxylink{class_trade_base_ac49916e7a2b3d5bfcbbf36d604b7d9f6}{price}, \doxylink{class_trade_base_a1ba68544e527dfd3b740e5798c706d8d}{change}, \doxylink{class_trade_base_a4dbbf63f2435bcb5eb44a16f20d44424}{size}, and \doxylink{class_trade_base_a95a99e75fc1594f819221b7f3217a316}{tick\+Direction} in the \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event, but they do update \doxylink{class_trade_base_a9a956f99368a4bdd97047d3ce8e80790}{day\+Volume} and \doxylink{class_trade_base_a6f9aff4fe560ba262c8bf524274a2408}{day\+Turnover}.

During extended trading hours a \doxylink{class_trade_e_t_h}{Trade\+ETH} event is generated on each trade with its \doxylink{class_trade_base_a8555f0cf2950d73e7b0f5bf2d1f70621}{extended\+Trading\+Hours} property set to {\ttfamily true}.

\doxysubsubsubsubsection*{Volume and Turnover}

The volume and turnover are included into the \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event instead of \doxylink{class_summary}{Summary} event, because both volume and turnover typically update with each trade. The \doxylink{class_trade_base_a49359e7e646d6916337477a298bef4ea}{day\+Id} field identifies current trading day for which volume and turnover statistics are computed. This solution avoids generation of multiple events on each trade during regular trading hours. \doxylink{class_summary}{Summary} event is generated during the trading day only when new highs or lows are reached or other properties change.

Note that one can compute volume-\/weighted average price (VWAP) for a day by this formula\+: ~\newline
{\ttfamily vwap = \doxylink{class_trade_base_a6f9aff4fe560ba262c8bf524274a2408}{day\+Turnover} / \doxylink{class_trade_base_a9a956f99368a4bdd97047d3ce8e80790}{day\+Volume};}

\doxysubsubsubsubsection*{Daily reset}

Daily reset procedure that happens on a schedule during non-\/trading hours resets \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} \doxylink{class_trade_base_a9a956f99368a4bdd97047d3ce8e80790}{day\+Volume} and \doxylink{class_trade_base_a6f9aff4fe560ba262c8bf524274a2408}{day\+Turnover} to math\+::\+NaN and sets \doxylink{class_trade_base_a49359e7e646d6916337477a298bef4ea}{day\+Id} to the next trading day in preparation to the next day\textquotesingle{}s pre-\/market trading session (or for regular trading if there is no pre-\/market) while leaving all other properties intact. They reflect information about the last known RTH trade until the next RTH trade happens.

\doxysubsubsubsubsection*{Implementation details}

This event is implemented on top of QDS records {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade}} and {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade}\&X} for regional exchange trades. Regional records do not explicitly store a field for \doxylink{class_trade_base_a5044e7359f50ff34f59c7ab201b1847b}{exchange\+Code} property.
