\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classdxfcpp_1_1_candle}{Candle} price level attribute of \doxylink{structdxfcpp_1_1_candle_symbol}{Candle\+Symbol} defines how candles shall be aggregated in respect to price interval. 

The negative or infinite values of price interval are treated as exceptional. 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Price interval may be equal to zero. It means every unique price creates a particular candle to aggregate all events with this price for the chosen \doxylink{structdxfcpp_1_1_candle_period}{Candle\+Period}. 
\item Non-\/zero price level creates sequence of intervals starting from 0\+: ...,\mbox{[}-\/pl;0),\mbox{[}0;pl),\mbox{[}pl;2\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}pl),...,\mbox{[}n\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}pl,n\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}pl+pl). Events aggregated by chosen \doxylink{structdxfcpp_1_1_candle_period}{Candle\+Period} and price intervals. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
