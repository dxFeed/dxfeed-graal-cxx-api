\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classCandle}{Candle}} price level attribute of \mbox{\hyperlink{structCandleSymbol}{Candle\+Symbol}} defines how candles shall be aggregated in respect to price interval. 

The negative or infinite values of price interval are treated as exceptional. 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Price interval may be equal to zero. It means every unique price creates a particular candle to aggregate all events with this price for the chosen \mbox{\hyperlink{structCandlePeriod}{Candle\+Period}}. 
\item Non-\/zero price level creates sequence of intervals starting from 0\+: ...,\mbox{[}-\/pl;0),\mbox{[}0;pl),\mbox{[}pl;2$\ast$pl),...,\mbox{[}n$\ast$pl,n$\ast$pl+pl). Events aggregated by chosen \mbox{\hyperlink{structCandlePeriod}{Candle\+Period}} and price intervals. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
