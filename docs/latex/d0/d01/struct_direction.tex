Current price is equal to the only known price value suitable for price direction computation. 

Unlike {\bfseries{UNDEFINED}} the {\bfseries{ZERO}} direction implies that current price is defined and direction computation was duly performed but has failed to detect any upward or downward price movement. It is also reported for cases when price sequence was broken and direction computation was restarted anew.
