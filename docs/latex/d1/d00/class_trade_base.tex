\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
Base class for common fields of \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade}\} and \doxylink{class_trade_e_t_h}{Trade\+ETH} events. 

\doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} events represent the most recent information that is available about the last trade on the market at any given moment of time.

\doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} event represents last trade information for {\bfseries{regular trading hours}} (RTH) with an official volume and turnover {\bfseries{for the whole trading day}} identified by \doxylink{class_trade_base_a49359e7e646d6916337477a298bef4ea}{day\+Id} field.

\doxylink{class_trade_e_t_h}{Trade\+ETH} event is defined only for symbols (typically stocks and ETFs) with a designated {\bfseries{extended trading hours}} (ETH, pre market and post market trading sessions). It represents last trade price during ETH and also accumulated volume and turnover during ETH for current trading day.
