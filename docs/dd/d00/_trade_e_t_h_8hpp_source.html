23/**
24 * TradeETH event is a snapshot of the price and size of the last trade during
25 * extended trading hours and the extended trading hours day volume and day turnover.
26 * This event is defined only for symbols (typically stocks and ETFs) with a designated
27 * <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH, pre market and post market trading sessions).
28 * It represents the most recent information that is available about
29 * ETH last trade on the market at any given moment of time.
30 *
31 * <h3>Trading sessions</h3>
32 *
33 * The TradeETH event defines last trade @ref TradeETH::getPrice() "price" as officially defined
34 * by the corresponding exchange for its <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH).
35 * It also includes @ref TradeETH::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref ::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover"
36 * <b>for the extended trading hours only</b> of the trading day identified by @ref TradeETH::getDayId "dayId".
37 * This event is not defined for symbols that has no concept of ETH.
38 *
39 * <p>When the first trade of <b>regular trading hours</b> (RTH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
40 * with @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `false`. Afterwards, during RTH,
41 * TradeETH event is not updated and retains information about the last trade, volume and turnover of the pre market
42 * trading session.
43 *
44 * <p>When the first trade of <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
45 * with @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `true`. Afterwards, during ETH,
46 * TradeETH event is updated on each trade with the last trade information from post market trading session
47 * and total volume and turnover of the pre and post market trading session (excluding the volume and turnover of a
48 * regular trading session).
49 *
50 * Note, that during pre- and post-market sessions, Trade event also updates, but only its
51 * @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" properties change
52 * to reflect the overall official volume and turnover as reported by exchanges.
53 * During post market trading session, exchanges may correct their official RTH last trading price, which results
54 * in the update to Trade event.
55 *
56 * <h3>Volume and Turnover</h3>
57 *
58 * <p>Note that one can compute volume-weighted average price (VWAP) for extended trading hours by this formula:
59 * <br><code>vwap = @ref TradeETH::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" / @ref ::getDayVolume() "dayVolume";</code>
60 *
61 * <h3>Daily reset</h3>
62 *
63 * Daily reset procedure that happens on a schedule during non-trading hours resets TradeETH
64 * @ref TradeETH::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref TradeETH::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" to math::NaN
65 * and sets @ref TradeETH::getDayId() "dayId" to the next trading day in preparation to the next day's pre-market
66 * trading session (or for regular trading if there is no pre-market) while leaving all other properties intact. They
67 * reflect information about the last known ETH trade until the next ETH trade happens.
68 *
69 * <h3>The most recent last trade price</h3>
70 *
71 * The most recent last trade price ("extended last price") in the market can be found by combining information from
72 * both Trade and TradeETH events using @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours" method to
73 * figure out which trading session had the most recent trade. The following piece of code finds the most recent last
74 * trade price from the given @ref DXFeed "feed" for a given `symbol`, assuming there is a @ref DXFeedSubscription
75 * "subscription" for both Trade and TradeETH events for the given `symbol`:
76 *
77 * <pre><tt>
78 * auto @ref Trade "trade" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(Trade::create(symbol));
79 * auto @ref TradeETH "tradeEth" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(TradeETH::create(symbol));
80 * <b>double</b> extLast = tradeEth->@ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours"() ?
81 * tradeEth->@ref TradeETH::getPrice() "getPrice"() : trade->@ref Trade::getPrice() "getPrice"();
82 * </tt></pre>
83 *
84 * Note, that the above code works correctly for symbols that has no concept of ETH, too, because in this
85 * case the DXFeed::getLastEvent() leaves default values in TradeETH
86 * event properties, which means that @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" flag is `false`
87 * and a regular Trade::getPrice() is used.
88 *
89 * <h3>Implementation details</h3>
90 *
91 * This event is implemented on top of QDS record `TradeETH` and `TradeETH&X`
92 * for regional exchange extended trade hours.
93 * @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property is internally represented as a last bit of
94 * the "Flags" field of the record. Regional records do not explicitly store a field for @ref
95 * TradeETH::getExchangeCode() "exchangeCode" property.
96 */
