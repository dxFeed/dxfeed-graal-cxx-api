23/**
24 * TradeETH event is a snapshot of the price and size of the last trade during
25 * extended trading hours and the extended trading hours day volume and day turnover.
26 * This event is defined only for symbols (typically stocks and ETFs) with a designated
27 * <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH, pre market and post market trading sessions).
28 * It represents the most recent information that is available about
29 * ETH last trade on the market at any given moment of time.
30 *
31 * <h3>Trading sessions</h3>
32 *
33 * The TradeETH event defines last trade @ref ::getPrice() "price" as officially defined
34 * by the corresponding exchange for its <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH).
35 * It also includes @ref ::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref ::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover"
36 * <b>for the extended trading hours only</b> of the trading day identified by @ref ::getDayId "dayId".
37 * This event is not defined for symbols that has no concept of ETH.
38 *
39 * <p>When the first trade of <b>regular trading hours</b> (RTH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
40 * with @ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `false`. Afterwards, during RTH,
41 * TradeETH event is not updated and retains information about the last trade, volume and turnover of the pre market trading session.
42 *
43 * <p>When the first trade of <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
44 * with @ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `true`. Afterwards, during ETH,
45 * TradeETH event is updated on each trade with the last trade information from post market trading session
46 * and total volume and turnover of the pre and post market trading session (excluding the volume and turnover of a regular trading session).
47 *
48 * Note, that during pre- and post-market sessions, Trade event also updates, but only its
49 * @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" properties change
50 * to reflect the overall official volume and turnover as reported by exchanges.
51 * During post market trading session, exchanges may correct their official RTH last trading price, which results
52 * in the update to Trade event.
53 *
54 * <h3>Volume and Turnover</h3>
55 *
56 * <p>Note that one can compute volume-weighted average price (VWAP) for extended trading hours by this formula:
57 * <br><code>vwap = @ref ::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" / @ref ::getDayVolume() "dayVolume";</code>
58 *
59 * <h3>Daily reset</h3>
60 *
61 * Daily reset procedure that happens on a schedule during non-trading hours resets TradeETH
62 * @ref #getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref ::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" to math::NaN
63 * and sets @ref ::getDayId() "dayId" to the next trading day in preparation to the next day's pre-market trading session
64 * (or for regular trading if there is no pre-market) while leaving all other properties intact.
65 * They reflect information about the last known ETH trade until the next ETH trade happens.
66 *
67 * <h3>The most recent last trade price</h3>
68 *
69 * The most recent last trade price ("extended last price") in the market can be found by combining information from both
70 * Trade and TradeETH events using @ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours" method to figure out
71 * which trading session had the most recent trade. The following piece of code finds the most
72 * recent last trade price from the given @ref DXFeed "feed" for a given `symbol`,
73 * assuming there is a @ref DXFeedSubscription "subscription" for both Trade and TradeETH events
74 * for the given `symbol`:
75 *
76 * <pre><tt>
77 * auto @ref Trade "trade" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(Trade::create(symbol));
78 * auto @ref TradeETH "tradeEth" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(TradeETH::create(symbol));
79 * <b>double</b> extLast = tradeEth->@ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours"() ? tradeEth->@ref ::getPrice() "getPrice"() : trade->@ref Trade::getPrice() "getPrice"();
80 * </tt></pre>
81 *
82 * Note, that the above code works correctly for symbols that has no concept of ETH, too, because in this
83 * case the DXFeed::getLastEvent() leaves default values in TradeETH
84 * event properties, which means that @ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" flag is `false`
85 * and a regular Trade::getPrice() is used.
86 *
87 * <h3>Implementation details</h3>
88 *
89 * This event is implemented on top of QDS record `TradeETH` and `TradeETH&X`
90 * for regional exchange extended trade hours.
91 * @ref ::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property is internally represented as a last bit of the "Flags" field of the record.
92 * Regional records do not explicitly store a field for @ref ::getExchangeCode() "exchangeCode" property.
93 */

95 friend struct EventMapper;
