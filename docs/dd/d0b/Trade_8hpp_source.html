23 /**
24  * Trade event is a snapshot of the price and size of the last trade during regular trading hours
25  * and an overall day volume and day turnover.
26  * It represents the most recent information that is available about the regular last trade on the market
27  * at any given moment of time.
28  *
29  * <h3>Trading sessions</h3>
30  *
31  * The Trade event defines last trade @ref Trade::getPrice() "price" as officially defined
32  * by the corresponding exchange for its <b>regular trading hours</b> (RTH).
33  * It also include an official exchange @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover()
34  * "dayTurnover" <b>for the whole trading day</b> identified by @ref Trade::getDayId() "dayId". So, Trade event captures
35  * all the official numbers that are typically reported by exchange.
36  *
37  * <p>Trades that happen in <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH, pre-market and post-market trading sessions),
38  * which are typically defined for stocks and ETFs, do not update last trade @ref Trade::getTime() "time",
39  * @ref Trade::getExchangeCode() "exchangeCode", @ref Trade::getPrice() "price", @ref Trade::getChange() "change",
40  * @ref Trade::getSize() "size", and @ref Trade::getTickDirection() "tickDirection" in the Trade event, but they do
41  * update
42  * @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover".
43  *
44  * <p>During extended trading hours a TradeETH event is generated on each trade with its
45  * @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `true`.</p>
46  *
47  * <h3>Volume and Turnover</h3>
48  *
49  * <p>The volume and turnover are included into the Trade event instead
50  * of Summary event, because both volume and turnover typically update with each trade.
51  * The @ref Trade::getDayId() "dayId" field identifies current trading day for which volume and turnover statistics are
52  * computed. This solution avoids generation of multiple events on each trade during regular trading hours. Summary
53  * event is generated during the trading day only when new highs or lows are reached or other properties change.
54  *
55  * <p>Note that one can compute volume-weighted average price (VWAP) for a day by this formula:
56  * <br><code>vwap = @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" / @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume";</code>
57  *
58  * <h3>Daily reset</h3>
59  *
60  * Daily reset procedure that happens on a schedule during non-trading hours resets Trade
61  * @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" to math::NaN
62  * and sets @ref Trade::getDayId() "dayId" to the next trading day in preparation to the next day's pre-market trading
63  * session (or for regular trading if there is no pre-market) while leaving all other properties intact. They reflect
64  * information about the last known RTH trade until the next RTH trade happens.
65  *
66  * <h3>Implementation details</h3>
67  *
68  * This event is implemented on top of QDS records `Trade` and `Trade&X`
69  * for regional exchange trades.
70  * Regional records do not explicitly store a field for @ref Trade::getExchangeCode() "exchangeCode" property.
71  */
