23/**
24 * Represents an extension of Order introducing analytic information, e.g. adding to this order iceberg related
25 * information (@ref AnalyticOrder::getIcebergPeakSize() "icebergPeakSize", @ref AnalyticOrder::getIcebergHiddenSize()
26 * "icebergHiddenSize",
27 * @ref AnalyticOrder::getIcebergExecutedSize() "icebergExecutedSize").
28 * The collection of analytic order events of a symbol represents the most recent analytic information
29 * that is available about orders on the market at any given moment of time.
30 *
31 * <p> Analytic order is similar to a regular Order. In addition this event has few additional properties:
32 * <ul>
33 * <li>@ref AnalyticOrder::getIcebergPeakSize() "icebergPeakSize" - the size of the peak, i.e. the visible part of
34 * the iceberg, that is being continually refilled until the order is fully traded or cancelled; <li>@ref
35 * AnalyticOrder::getIcebergHiddenSize() "icebergHiddenSize" - the prediction of current hidden size of the iceberg, as
36 * inferred by the model; <li>@ref AnalyticOrder::getIcebergExecutedSize() "icebergExecutedSize" - the executed size of
37 * the iceberg order. For IcebergType::SYNTHETIC type represents total executed size of all orders composing current
38 * iceberg; <li>AnalyticOrder::getIcebergType() - type of the iceberg, either native (exchange-managed) or synthetic
39 * (managed outside of the exchange).
40 * </ul>
41 *
42 * <h3>Implementation details</h3>
43 * <p>
44 * This event is implemented on top of QDS records `AnalyticOrder#<source-id>`,
45 * where `<source-id>` is up to 4 ASCII characters with a mnemonic for the source like "GLBX".
46 */
