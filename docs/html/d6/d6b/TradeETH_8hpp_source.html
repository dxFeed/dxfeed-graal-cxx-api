25 /**
26  * TradeETH event is a snapshot of the price and size of the last trade during
27  * extended trading hours and the extended trading hours day volume and day turnover.
28  * This event is defined only for symbols (typically stocks and ETFs) with a designated
29  * <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH, pre market and post market trading sessions).
30  * It represents the most recent information that is available about
31  * ETH last trade on the market at any given moment of time.
32  *
33  * <h3>Trading sessions</h3>
34  *
35  * The TradeETH event defines last trade @ref TradeETH::getPrice() "price" as officially defined
36  * by the corresponding exchange for its <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH).
37  * It also includes @ref TradeETH::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref ::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover"
38  * <b>for the extended trading hours only</b> of the trading day identified by @ref TradeETH::getDayId "dayId".
39  * This event is not defined for symbols that has no concept of ETH.
40  *
41  * <p>When the first trade of <b>regular trading hours</b> (RTH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
42  * with @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `false`. Afterwards, during RTH,
43  * TradeETH event is not updated and retains information about the last trade, volume and turnover of the pre market
44  * trading session.
45  *
46  * <p>When the first trade of <b>extended trading hours</b> (ETH) happens, then TradeETH event is generated
47  * with @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property set to `true`. Afterwards, during ETH,
48  * TradeETH event is updated on each trade with the last trade information from post market trading session
49  * and total volume and turnover of the pre and post market trading session (excluding the volume and turnover of a
50  * regular trading session).
51  *
52  * Note, that during pre- and post-market sessions, Trade event also updates, but only its
53  * @ref Trade::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref Trade::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" properties change
54  * to reflect the overall official volume and turnover as reported by exchanges.
55  * During post market trading session, exchanges may correct their official RTH last trading price, which results
56  * in the update to Trade event.
57  *
58  * <h3>Volume and Turnover</h3>
59  *
60  * <p>Note that one can compute volume-weighted average price (VWAP) for extended trading hours by this formula:
61  * <br><code>vwap = @ref TradeETH::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" / @ref ::getDayVolume() "dayVolume";</code>
62  *
63  * <h3>Daily reset</h3>
64  *
65  * Daily reset procedure that happens on a schedule during non-trading hours resets TradeETH
66  * @ref TradeETH::getDayVolume() "dayVolume" and @ref TradeETH::getDayTurnover() "dayTurnover" to math::NaN
67  * and sets @ref TradeETH::getDayId() "dayId" to the next trading day in preparation to the next day's pre-market
68  * trading session (or for regular trading if there is no pre-market) while leaving all other properties intact. They
69  * reflect information about the last known ETH trade until the next ETH trade happens.
70  *
71  * <h3>The most recent last trade price</h3>
72  *
73  * The most recent last trade price ("extended last price") in the market can be found by combining information from
74  * both Trade and TradeETH events using @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours" method to
75  * figure out which trading session had the most recent trade. The following piece of code finds the most recent last
76  * trade price from the given @ref DXFeed "feed" for a given `symbol`, assuming there is a @ref DXFeedSubscription
77  * "subscription" for both Trade and TradeETH events for the given `symbol`:
78  *
79  * <pre><tt>
80  * auto @ref Trade "trade" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(Trade::create(symbol));
81  * auto @ref TradeETH "tradeEth" = feed->@ref DXFeed::getLastEvent() "getLastEvent"(TradeETH::create(symbol));
82  * <b>double</b> extLast = tradeEth->@ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "isExtendedTradingHours"() ?
83  * tradeEth->@ref TradeETH::getPrice() "getPrice"() : trade->@ref Trade::getPrice() "getPrice"();
84  * </tt></pre>
85  *
86  * Note, that the above code works correctly for symbols that has no concept of ETH, too, because in this
87  * case the DXFeed::getLastEvent() leaves default values in TradeETH
88  * event properties, which means that @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" flag is `false`
89  * and a regular Trade::getPrice() is used.
90  *
91  * <h3>Implementation details</h3>
92  *
93  * This event is implemented on top of QDS record `TradeETH` and `TradeETH&X`
94  * for regional exchange extended trade hours.
95  * @ref TradeETH::isExtendedTradingHours() "extendedTradingHours" property is internally represented as a last bit of
96  * the "Flags" field of the record. Regional records do not explicitly store a field for @ref
97  * TradeETH::getExchangeCode() "exchangeCode" property.
98  */
